About Our Firm

A Tradition of
Tenacious Advocacy

Founded in 1997 by Eleanor Prescott, our firm has earned a reputation for fearless courtroom advocacy and meticulous case preparation. What began as a solo practice has grown into a team of fourteen attorneys across two offices.

Our lawyers have tried over 350 cases to verdict and recovered hundreds of millions for clients. We combine the tenacity of trial lawyers with the analytical rigor of scholars, and we never back down from a fight worth fighting.
