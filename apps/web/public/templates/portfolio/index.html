ABOUT ME

Designing with purpose since 2017

I'm Maya Lindgren, a Stockholm-based creative director specializing in brand identity, product design, and visual storytelling. I believe great design isn't about decoration — it's about clarity, emotion, and trust.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring Scandinavian forests, experimenting with ceramics, or mentoring young designers at Hyper Island.
