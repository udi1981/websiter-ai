Our Offerings

Classes & Practices

Vinyasa Flow

INTERMEDIATE • 60 MIN

Dynamic, breath-synchronized movement that builds heat, strength, and flexibility through flowing sequences.

Hatha Yoga

ALL LEVELS • 75 MIN

A gentle, foundational practice focusing on proper alignment, breathing techniques, and held postures.

Yin Yoga

ALL LEVELS • 90 MIN

Slow, meditative practice with long-held floor poses that deeply stretch connective tissues and calm the mind.

Hot Yoga

INTERMEDIATE • 60 MIN

Practiced in a heated room to deepen stretches, promote detoxification, and increase cardiovascular endurance.

Guided Meditation

ALL LEVELS • 45 MIN

Cultivate mindfulness and inner peace through guided breathwork, visualization, and seated meditation techniques.

Prenatal Yoga

SPECIALIZED • 60 MIN

Safe, nurturing practice designed for expecting mothers with modified poses, breathing, and relaxation.
